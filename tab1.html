Predicting Role Meta?

Will Machine learning be able to predict the Meta of a League of Legends season, Based on roles?

What is a Meta?

In short, the metagame in video gaming means using characters or items that are the most powerful at the time to try and find the best and quickest means to victory.



Though testing over 300 teams, we have had the following results:

Machine learning tree focused on Role selection of the winning team in League of Legends. Roughly 72% prediction rate.

Machine learning tree focused on champion selection of the winning team in League of Legends. Roughly 50% prediction rate.
