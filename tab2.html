Looking at the Numbers of the "Meta"

The image above shows us the average pick rate of each role for the winning and losing team. This information is useful to us, however it does not show the complete story on its own.


Winners Meta ?

The winning team tended to value marksmen, fighters, and assassins more than the losing team.

Losers Meta ?

The losing team tended to over-value support and mages relative to the winning team.


Although the previous data gives us a decent idea of the current meta of League of Legends, it doesn't show us the full picture. This data can't tell us what specific line up will be effective against another. To answer this question, we used machine learning.



Our machine learning algorithm was able to predict the winner of a match with 72% accuracy. The image above show the prediction strength for each variable (doubled to show the effect for each team). We can see here that the most important roles for predicting the outcome of a game are the Assassin and Mage. Using our knowledge from the first image, we can conclude that the undervalueing of assassins and the overvalueing of mages are the two biggest factors in predicting the outcome of a game.
